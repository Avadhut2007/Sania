✦ A Letter From the Heart ✦

My Dear Baby Sania,

It's hard for me to believe that it's already been one month of us again. One month of loving you openly, one month of having you, one month of feeling like my heart finally found its peace again after such a long time.

And honestly, that peace comes from you.

"You have this way of comforting me that nobody else ever could."

Even on my worst days, your words calm something inside me so naturally. Kabhi kabhi you don't even realize it, but the smallest things you do — the way you talk to me, the way you listen, the way you stay — they heal me in ways I can never fully explain.

I don't think you truly understand how much I love you. This isn't some temporary feeling for me. It's not something that changes with mood or time. The love I have for you is immense, deeper than anything I've ever felt in my life.

Tu abb mera hissa bann chuki hai, you're not even an individual person, bas tera naam Sania hai but tu fully meri hai aur apan ekk haii. ♡

"Even after everything, we still chose us. And that's what I love about us!"

And yes, we've had our ups and downs. We've argued, misunderstood each other, overthought things, and hurt each other at times. But what matters to me is that even after everything, we still chose us. We still found our way back to each other. And that's what I love about us!

No relationship is perfect, and ours won't be either. There will be difficult days, stressful moments, mood swings, distance, and fights. But no matter what comes our way, I want you to know this — I'm always going to be here for you and us.

I'm not loving you for a short chapter of my life. I'm loving you for the long run. For every phase, every version of us, every future moment we haven't even lived yet. I love you more than anything.

"When I look at you, I don't see something temporary. I see home. I see my future wife."

And maybe it sounds crazy to some people, but when I look at you, I don't see something temporary. I see home. I see my future wife. I see the person I want beside me years from now when life gets real and difficult and beautiful all at once.

And honestly? I can't wait for the day we finally get married and look back at all of this together.

This is our first month together, Again! But it's been so long since I love you — this is just the start!

Thank you for loving me.
Thank you for staying.
Thank you for being you.

Happy one month anniversary, my baby.
I love you more than words will ever be enough for.

Yours,

Avadhut ♡
